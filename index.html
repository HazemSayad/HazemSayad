What Are Some of My Thoughts?

I have many ideas and opinions, so here are some snippets of my mind:

  • Technology has proven itself to be essential in everyday's life; Engineers use tools. In the yester-years, they used hard tools, while in the modern era, I must use technology as it is a powerful and versatile tool, just like a swiss army pocket knife!
  • Many people confuse Experience and Knowledge. People are not fully wrong, and here is why. Experience is the simple sum of: Knowledge + Consistency.

    The beauty of Knowledge is that it can be SHARED! That exactly means a 100 years old man and a 15 year old boy with the ability to learn, can achieve the same ammount of knowledge. Which brings us to

    Consistency. This part is so simple yet powerful as well. It is like hammering a nail, sure the knowledge is common, but the consistency lies in the accuracy and success rate. Though this is only a matter of repetition and time to achieve a comparable end result. And here is where knowledge beats experience: Trying to hammer a screw!
  • So many people give up on this world, believing that it is a lost cause, a failed world, or even a slowly sinking ship. Well maybe. I don't seek comfort in lies to the self, and I thrive on the potential to change and improving. WE can do better!
  • Knowledge is power, but those in power often forget that it is a responsibility, and they abuse it. Use what's in your power to do good and help.
  • I love sharing knowledge, it is free, useful and often helpful!
