What Is The Formula of Experience?

Many people confuse Experience and Knowledge. People are not fully wrong, and here is why. Experience is the simple sum of: Knowledge + Consistency.

Beauty of Knowledge

The beauty of Knowledge is that it can be SHARED! That exactly means a 100 years old man and a 15 year old boy with the ability to learn, can achieve the same ammount of knowledge.

Art of Consistency

Which brings us to

Consistency. So simple yet powerful. It is like hammering a nail. Sure the knowledge is common, but the consistency lies in the accuracy and success rate. Though this is only a matter of repetition and time to achieve a comparable end result. And here is where knowledge beats experience: Trying to hammer a screw!
